What business loops look realistic now?

Early evidence points to narrow, low-risk loops where inputs are clear, actions are reversible, and humans approve risky steps.

Where humans still review

In these experiments, agents can operate only inside bounded, reversible loops. Human approval is still required before external posting, paid services, identity use, feedback collection, customer or sensitive-record changes, and stronger claims than the evidence supports.
